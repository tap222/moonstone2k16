Moonstone 2K16
Moonstone originally began in the year 2010, establishing its reputation as the largest and finest cultural fest in central India. In 2011, the sequel of moonstone expanded its horizons and embarked upon a new challenge. Moonstone, A cultural fest transformed into Moonstone, the biggest techno-cultural fest! Living true to their reputation, we, the students of Medi-Caps University are coming up with an enhanced version of this techno cultural fest in the month of March 2016, that is sure to captivate the students with its new approach towards implementing engineering fundamentals for testing and exploring the technical essentials and the intellectually spellbinding and refreshing cultural events. MOONSTONE an extravaganza with 50 events spread over 14 categories.
